Aeon

Aeon


Aeon adalah makhluk misterius berdimensi lebih tinggi seperti dewa di Honkai: Star Rail yang memimpin alam semesta. Setiap Aeon dikaitkan dengan sebuah Path, yang dengannya mereka dapat menggunakan energinya sesuka mereka, namun mereka juga terikat padanya selama sisa hidup mereka.

People know very little about these mysterious existences that stroll through the vast stellar sea. Limited by inherent capacities of cognition, intelligent lifeforms can only faintly discern that Aeons tread upon Paths imperceptible to mortal beings and execute unfathomable powers via some form of belief. In legends passed down through word of mouth, the Aeons became the embodiment of highly condensed philosophical concepts in the end.

If anyone finds themselves treading on the Path of an Aeon, they will be inundated by a distant sensation, like being struck by a gaze from light-years across the cosmos. Many believe that this is the only connection the Aeons and mortals can ever have.

— Data Bank, Aeons

They call them, "Aeons" in this cosmos. They construct reality, erase stars, and leave their marks on countless worlds. Together with your companions, you’ll travel across the cosmos on the Astral Express, following the path once traveled by the Aeons. From here, you will explore new civilizations, meet new comrades, and begin new adventures among countless fantastical worlds. All the answers you seek will be uncovered among the stars. What are you waiting for? Are you ready to begin this trailblazing journey?

— Official website introduction
